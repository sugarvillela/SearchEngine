UC Davis Parents Fund

Your Parents Fund gift supports all aspects of students' undergraduate education including scholarships, classroom upgrades, student-run projects and faculty awards. Parents Fund gifts of $1,000 or more are recognized by the Davis Chancellor's Club (DCC).
